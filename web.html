About Us

We nurture our plants so they can nurture your soul. From our greenhouses - spread over 25 acres - to your gardens, we plant seeds of peace and serenity in plant form. We believe that gardening is therapy. So, we bring the best of all gardening and plant care equipment to you, to ensure that you and your green companions never fall short of anything!

We believe that plants grow people. We are here to inspire a world where every plant journey is a personal revolution of inner change and growth. As a gardening brand, we also exist to make your gardening journey as seamless as possible.
